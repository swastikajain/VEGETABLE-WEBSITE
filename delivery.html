Delivery Information

We strive to deliver your fresh vegetables in a timely and efficient manner. Please read the following delivery information to understand our delivery process:

1. Delivery Zones

We currently offer delivery services within the following zones:

  • Dehradun: Within the city limits.
  • Delhi : Suburbs and nearby areas.
  • Bareilly C: Outlying areas.

2. Delivery Charges

Delivery charges vary based on the delivery zone and order value:

  • Dehradun: Free delivery on orders above Rs.300. Rs40 delivery fee for orders below Rs.30.
  • Delhi: Free delivery on orders above Rs.800. Rs.100 delivery fee for orders below Rs.60.
  • Bareilly: Free delivery on orders above Rs.500.Rs.120 delivery fee for orders below Rs.80.

3. Delivery Time

Our delivery team operates on all days of the week from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. You can choose a preferred delivery time during the checkout process. However, please note that delivery timings are subject to availability and may vary based on order volumes and traffic conditions.

4. Delivery Tracking

For your convenience, we provide order tracking. Once your order is out for delivery, you will receive a notification with a tracking link to monitor the status of your delivery.

5. Order Changes and Cancellations

If you wish to make changes to your order or cancel it, please contact our customer support team at least 2 hours before the scheduled delivery time. Changes and cancellations made within 2 hours of the scheduled delivery time may not be possible.
