Return Policy

Thank you for shopping with us at Online Vegetable Market. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you may return the items as per the following return policy:

1. Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, the item(s) must be:

  • Returned within 7 days of receiving the order.
  • In its original condition and packaging.
  • Unopened and unused.

2. How to Initiate a Return

If you wish to return an item, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer support team at support@vegetablemarket.com within 7 days of receiving the order.
  • Provide your order number, reason for the return, and any relevant details.
  • Our customer support team will guide you through the return process and provide you with return instructions.

3. Return Shipping

The customer is responsible for the return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping method to ensure that the returned items reach us.

4. Refunds

Once we receive the returned items and verify their condition, we will process the refund. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Please allow up to 10 business days for the refund to reflect in your account.

5. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are non-returnable:

  • Perishable items such as fresh vegetables.
  • Items that are not in their original condition or have been opened or used.
