Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

To place an order, follow these simple steps:

  • Step 1: Browse our selection of fresh vegetables.
  • Step 2: Add the items you want to purchase to your shopping cart.
  • Step 3: Proceed to checkout and provide your delivery details.
  • Step 4: Make the payment using our secure payment options.
  • Step 5: Sit back and relax. Your fresh vegetables will be delivered to your doorstep!

2. What are the delivery charges?

We offer free delivery on all orders above $30. For orders below $30, a nominal delivery fee of $5 will be applied.
